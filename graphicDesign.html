Ketab Font

Ketab is a geometric sans serif font I created, inspired by the arabic alphabet. Ketab is intended to marry the harsh structure of english letters with the fluidity of arabic typography.

Pocket Barista Logo

Pocket Barista is a desktop application/turned iOS application created by a team of peers and I. Inspired by our part-time barista jobs, the application matches users with a coffee drink for them. This is the logo designed for the application.

STEM Expo Poster

This is a poster created with a customized type face for the annual STEM Expo held at our school to expose the local girl scouts to various fields in STEM.

Stain Type

This is a custom typeface I designed to embody the meaning of the word "stain" into the actual desing of the font. I used a coffee stain as inspiration.

Monogram

This monogram is a design of my initials inspired by a Rubik's Cube. The famous toy is a symbol of my introduction to STEM since it was one of my greatest influences before college.

Gizmo Logo

Gizmo is a fictional company that creates toys for children. This logo was designed to represent the fun products of the Gizmo corporation.
